MWDOC Divison 7

Vote for Megan Yoo Schneider to continue to serve as the Division 7 Director on the Municipal Water District of Orange County (MWDOC) Board of Directors. Division 7 includes Aliso Viejo, Dana Point, Laguna Niguel, Laguna Hills, San Clemente, Mission Viejo, Laguna Beach, and San Juan Capistrano.

Retail water agencies in the MWDOC Division 7 area include Emerald Bay Services District, Laguna Beach County Water District, Moulton Niguel Water District, Santa Margarita Water District, and South Coast Water District..
